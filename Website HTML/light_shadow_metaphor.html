The Light and Shadow Principle

THE LIGHT
Source of Coherence

• Self-existing
• Creates illumination
• Reveals truth
• Primary reality
• Cannot create darkness
THE SHADOW
Decoherence Effect

• Not a thing itself
• Absence of light
• Reveals obstruction
• Secondary phenomenon
• Dependent on light to exist
THE OBSTRUCTION
Blocking Agent

• Creates the shadow
• Finite and removable
• Not inherent to light
• Source of the problem
• Can be moved or dissolved

The Key Insight

When you see a shadow, you don't conclude the sun is defective or that darkness is a creative force. You understand that something is blocking the light. Similarly, evil in the world doesn't disprove ultimate goodness—it proves something is obstructing it.
